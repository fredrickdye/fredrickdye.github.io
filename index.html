I'm frequently asked how I got where I am, career-wise. It's a simple answer but folks usually look at me like I'm an space alien. I try to be more today than I was yesterday.

When I started out, the bosses were still three-ring binder people. In fact, most of the folks who reported to me early on were paper people. I've solved problems and pushed forward for a long time.

I've been fortunate, too. I've worked for heavyweight brands and amazing people. I look for a challenge and people who want to be the best at what they, and their organizations, do.

We've simplified things around here. I don't do any public-facing work these days and I was in a distillation mood at the time. Things are still in transition but you can find the old samples over at news.freddye.com for the time being. I may add some few recent things but there is some basic information about my regular work here. If you need more, let me know.
